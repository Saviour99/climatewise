Who We Are

ClimateWISE Youth Organization (CYO) is a dynamic non-profit, youth-led organization committed to advancing climate resilience, water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH), and sustainable development in communities. We focus on empowering youth, women, and vulnerable groups through education, capacity building, and skills development.

Education & Empowerment

Equipping youth and vulnerable groups with knowledge, skills, and leadership to drive community transformation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every action, big or small, contributes to the well-being of people, communities, and the environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to mobilize and equip youth and vulnerable groups to lead climate and WASH solutions that protect ecosystems, confront environmental injustices, and strengthen community resilience.

We do this through education, community-based action, research, and partnerships that strengthen livelihoods, protect ecosystems, and build healthier, more resilient communities.
