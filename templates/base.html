About Green Growth Africa

Empowering Communities for a Sustainable Future

Our Mission

We are a dynamic non-profit, non-governmental organization dedicated to catalyzing socio-economic, environmental, and technological growth in Africa's rural and urban poor communities.

Through education, capacity building, and skills training, we are empowering communities to take charge of their own development and create a brighter future for themselves and future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every action, big or small, contributes to the well-being of both people and the environment. A thriving African community that operates within a circular economy.

Our focus is centered on vulnerable groups, such as women and youth, creating sustainable pathways for development and growth.

Thematic Areas

Each area is meticulously designed to address specific facets of community growth

Socio-Economic Empowerment

Green Growth Africa (GGA) fosters socio-economic empowerment in Africa by addressing poverty, unemployment, and financial exclusion. In Ghana, where poverty was 23.4% in 2017, GGA works to create opportunities for vulnerable groups, particularly women and youth.

Through skills training, capacity building, and entrepreneurship programs, we help communities achieve financial independence and sustainable livelihoods. Our initiatives focus on providing access to resources, markets, and training that enable individuals to start and grow businesses.

Agriculture and Biodiversity

Green Growth Africa promotes sustainable agriculture and biodiversity conservation through research, advocacy and capacity building on emerging agricultural trends, species management, and ecosystem preservation.

We work with farming communities to implement climate-smart agricultural practices, preserve indigenous crop varieties, and promote sustainable land management techniques that enhance productivity while protecting biodiversity.

Environment and Climate Change

Africa is severely faced with the stiffening effects of the triple planetary crises of climate change, biodiversity loss and pollution needing urgent conservation and climate action.

GGA implements climate adaptation and mitigation strategies, promotes renewable energy solutions, and advocates for policies that protect our environment. We work with communities to build resilience against climate impacts while reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

Circular Economy

Green Growth Africa champions the transition to a circular economy by promoting waste reduction, recycling, and sustainable production. Through innovation and education, GGA empowers communities to rethink waste management.

We facilitate the development of circular economy models that transform waste into resources, creating economic opportunities while reducing environmental impact. Our programs support waste management enterprises and recycling initiatives.

Ecotourism and Community Development

Green Growth Africa (GGA) promotes ecotourism in Ghana as a sustainable way to boost local economies while preserving biodiversity. Ecotourism creates opportunities for communities to benefit from natural resources without depleting them.

We work with local communities to develop tourism initiatives that celebrate culture, protect wildlife, and generate sustainable income. Our approach ensures that tourism benefits are distributed equitably within communities.

Our Projects

Each project is a step forward in building a better, more sustainable future

Socio-Economic Empowerment (SEE) Accra Project

Accra's high population density presents challenges including environmental degradation, pollution, and social inequality. Green Growth Africa addresses these through skills training and entrepreneurship development.

Fisheries Empowerment Project (FEP Ghana)

The fisheries sector has played a significant role in Ghana's economy. This project empowers fishing communities with sustainable practices, value addition techniques, and market access.

Saving The Forest Elephants Project (SFEP)

The majestic forest elephant, a keystone species in Ghana's ecosystems, faces extinction. This project works to reduce human-elephant conflict while preserving elephant populations.

Coastal Resilience & Sustainable Livelihood Initiative (CRESLI)

Climate change impacts coastal communities severely. This project builds resilience through climate adaptation strategies, sustainable fishing practices, and alternative livelihoods.

Rising Roots Project

Green Growth Africa seeks to harness the potential of young innovators and atypical entrepreneurs through mentorship, training, and access to resources for sustainable business development.

Beyond Waste Initiative

Ghana's waste management faces challenges including inadequate infrastructure. This initiative promotes circular economy principles and transforms waste into valuable resources.

The Northern Ghana Climate Resilience Project (NGCR)

The Northern part of Ghana is recognized as the food basket but is heavily affected by climatic factors. This project strengthens resilience through sustainable agriculture and water management.

Palm Oil Value Addition Project (POVAP)

Palm oil production is crucial income for many farmers. This project enhances value addition and promotes sustainable palm oil production practices while protecting biodiversity.

Promoting Social Protection for All (SPA)

Green Growth Africa recognises the importance of women's economic empowerment and the potential it holds for sustainable development and social protection initiatives.
